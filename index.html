Progressive overload
What is progressive overload?
Progressive overload is the gradual increase of stress placed on the muscles over time to stimulate strength and muscle growth. It is one of the most fundamental principles of resistance training.
How does it work?
When you lift weights or exercise, your muscles experience stress. If the stress is slightly increased over time, your muscles adapt by growing stronger, bigger, or more enduring. Without progression, your muscles plateau and stop improving.
Supplements
What are supplements?
Supplements are concentrated forms of nutrients (protein, vitamins, minerals, or performance enhancers) that help fill dietary gaps. They are meant to support, not replace, a healthy diet.
Are supplements bad for me?
No, supplements contain natural or essential nutrients, Most supplements contain vitamins, minerals, amino acids, or plant-based compounds that your body already uses. They are just concentrated for convenience.
